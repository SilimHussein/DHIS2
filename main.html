Pecorino and thyme crostini

  • 150g grated Pecorino Romano cheese
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon fresh choped thyme leaves
  • 1 (200g) tub cream cheese, softened
  • 1 baguette
  1. Preheat the grill. Slice baguette into 1cm slices. Arrange the bread slices on a baking tray. Toast under the grill for 1 minute per side, or until both sides are slightly toasted.
  2. In a mixing bowl, combine the Pecorino, garlic, thyme and cream cheese. Spread the mixture over the tops of the baguette slices. Grill for a further 3 minutes, until cheese is melted.

Custard Shorties

  • 6 ozs Self Raising Flour
  • 2 ozs Custard Powder
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 4 ozs Butter can use Flora Buttery
  • 4 ozs Caster sugar
  • 1 egg beaten with 2 tbsp Milk
  • Your favourite jam

instructions

  1. Oven heat Gas Mk 4 /180c/350f
  2. Sieve together Flour, baking powder and custard powder
  3. Rub in butter (as for pastry)
  4. Stir in sugar
  5. Bind together with beaten egg and milk adding more milk if too dry
  6. Roll out lightly on floured surface to a thickness of 1.5 cms or 3/4 inch.
  7. Using 6cm - 2 1/2 inch pastry cutter cut into rounds flouring cutter between each one 12 - 18 rounds
  8. Place cake cases in patty pans
  9. Centre one round in each case
  10. Put half a teaspoonful of jam in the well
  11. Bake in the centre of preheated oven for 20 mins till golden brown showing an eye of jam in centre
  12. cool and dust with sifted icing sugar if desired.
